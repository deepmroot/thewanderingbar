We are an upscale mobile bar service based in Kamloops, BC, specializing in handcrafted cocktails, refined mocktails, specialty coffee, and curated beverage experiences designed to complement your event.

Whether it's an intimate gathering or a large celebration, we bring a polished, professional bar experience directly to you. Our focus is simple — exceptional drinks, thoughtful presentation, and seamless service.

The Person Behind the Bar

Meet Gloria,
founder & creator.

I have always had a passion for bringing people together and creating memorable experiences. Hosting parties and gatherings in our home several times a year has allowed me to express my creativity, attention to detail, and love for entertaining.

Over the years I realized that event planning is more than a hobby — it’s something I truly enjoy and feel naturally drawn to. When the opportunity arose to purchase this business, I saw it as the perfect way to turn my passion into a profession.

I love helping clients plan their events, bringing their vision to life while adding my own expertise and creative touch. Whether it’s a celebration, milestone event, or special occasion — every event deserves a special touch. From intimate gatherings to grand celebrations, our goal is to create experiences your guests will remember long after the last drink is poured.

How It Works

What is
Dry-Hire?

We bring the bar, you bring the booze. Full setup, professional service, zero stress — perfect for weddings, birthdays, or any celebration.

You Supply the Drinks

Choose your favourite wines, spirits, and beers. Buy exactly what you need — no waste, no markup.

We Handle Everything Else

Professional bartending, beautiful setup, custom cocktail menus, garnishes, and seamless service all night.

No Alcohol Stress You choose the drinks; we handle the rest.
Budget-Friendly Pay for what you want, not a pre-set package.
Zero Legal Headaches We serve, you supply — no licence worries.
